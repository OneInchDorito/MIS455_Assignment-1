An indie game, short for independent video game, is a video game created by individuals or smaller development teams without the financial and technical support of a large game publisher, in contrast to most "AAA" (triple-A) games. In 2024, Indie games are on the rise. Compared to the AAA titles, indie titles have gained quite the tracktion as they offer more value for their produts at lower prices. Some of the best idnie titles are Lethal Company, night-runners, etc. These are indie titles made by only one developer. This Website is dedicated to these two amazing indie titles
